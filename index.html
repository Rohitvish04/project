About TARC Kailāsa

Crafting Iconic Spaces

TARC Kailāsa is an architectural sanctuary where harmony, space and modern sophistication come together. Located on Patel Road, West Delhi, it offers a rare opportunity to elevate your lifestyle without leaving your world behind.

Thoughtfully designed Large Format Residences blend scale, elegance and contemporary comfort. With 1,70,000 sq.ft. of amenities, exclusive lifestyle upgrades and six layers of landscape greens, you're at an address where nature and indulgence coexist gracefully.
